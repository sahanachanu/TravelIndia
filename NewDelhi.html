Transporation Modes in New Delhi
Metro Trains
It is fast, efficient, fairly priced and environmentally friendly.Currently there are 6 lines around the city covering many stations. A route map is available at Metro Map.. A fare chart is available at Metro Fares..
Buses
The buses are usually overcrowded with little sitting room, and very hot if not airconditioned. They are however cheap and service many routes.The bus routes with bus numbers can be found at Bus Route.
Taxi/Cab
The most comfortable way to get around New Delhi is by taxi, however this is the most expensive option as well. Look for Cabs at Taxi/Cabs.
Auto Rikshaws
Auto is another form of transportation which are cheap and convenient to travel in.3-4 people can sit in one auto with a driver.Autos can be found anywhere.
Climate in New Delhi
SUMMER - April to July (extremely hot 25 to 45 degree celsius)
WINTER - December to January (extremely cold 5 to 20 degree celcius)
